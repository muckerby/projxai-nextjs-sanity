## HOW IT WORKS

Headline: The process

Step 1 — Discovery Call (Free, 30 min):
We learn about your business, your goals, and your current AI experience. You leave with 3 actionable insights regardless of whether we work together.

Step 2 — Business Audit (3-5 business days):
We analyse your operations in depth — your processes, your team structure, your current tools, and your competitive landscape. We identify where AI can deliver measurable results.

Step 3 — Roadmap Delivery:
We present your AI Implementation Roadmap in a 60-minute working session — walking through priorities, rationale, and next steps. You receive the full document to keep.

Step 4 — Optional: Implementation Support:
Many clients continue with us for implementation — building the tools, workflows, and systems identified in the roadmap. Quoted separately based on scope.
