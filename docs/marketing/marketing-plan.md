## Overview

A 90-day marketing plan designed to test all four income streams simultaneously with minimal ad spend. The philosophy: launch fast, measure everything, double down on what converts, cut what doesn't. No brand awareness campaigns. Every dollar must be traceable to a lead or a sale.

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1-4, pre-ad spend)

Everything in Phase 1 costs $0 in ad spend. It's about getting the site and tracking right before spending money driving traffic to it.

### 1.1 Site readiness checklist (Session 3-4)
- [ ] All five main pages live and complete
- [ ] ROAS Calculator built and email gate working
- [ ] Email platform connected (ConvertKit recommended — free up to 1,000 subscribers)
- [ ] Google Analytics 4 installed and verified
- [ ] Goal tracking configured: calculator_completed, email_submitted, work_with_us_cta_clicked
- [ ] Meta Pixel installed (needed before running Meta ads)
- [ ] Heatmap tool installed: Microsoft Clarity (free) — understand how visitors behave

### 1.2 SEO foundation
- Google Search Console: verify projxai.com.au — submit sitemap
- Google Business Profile: set up for ProjxAI / Collicorp in Brisbane
- Each page has unique title tag, meta description, H1 — no duplicates
- Blog: publish first 2 posts before spending on ads (gives Google something to index)

### 1.3 LinkedIn presence (organic, free)
Michael posts 3x per week on personal LinkedIn:
- Post 1 type: Short insight — "One AI thing Australian SMEs should know this week"
- Post 2 type: Behind the build — sharing what ProjxAI is building and why
- Post 3 type: Question or poll — engage the audience, learn what resonates
Target: 500 targeted connections in 90 days (Australian SME owners, marketing managers, agency owners)
No LinkedIn Ads until organic is working.

### 1.4 Email list seeding
Before the ROAS Calculator is live, build a small seed list:
- Personal network: anyone who might be a ProjxAI prospect
- LinkedIn connections who engage with posts
- Target: 50 subscribers before first email goes out
First email: announce the ROAS Calculator launch

## Phase 2 — ROAS Calculator Launch (Weeks 5-8)

### 2.1 Launch sequence

Week 5:
- ROAS Calculator live on /tools/roas-calculator
- LinkedIn post: announce the tool (personal post from Michael — not a brand post)
- Email seed list: "I built something — check it out"
- Submit to Product Hunt (free listing — Australian audience)
- Post in relevant Australian Facebook groups (SME owners, eCommerce groups)

Week 6-8:
- Monitor: calculator completion rate, email capture rate, affiliate link click rate
- A/B test: hero headline on calculator page
- If email capture rate > 30%: scale traffic to the page
- If < 20%: revisit the email gate value proposition

### 2.2 Paid traffic test — ROAS Calculator (Week 6)
Budget: $100-150 AUD for a 2-week test
Platform: Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
Audience: Australian business owners, 30-55, interested in digital marketing, eCommerce
Ad format: single image or short video
Ad copy direction: "Is your ad spend actually working? Find out free in 2 minutes."
Landing page: /tools/roas-calculator
Goal: email capture
Target CPA: under $5 per email address

If CPA is under $5 and affiliate clicks are converting: scale budget.

## Phase 3 — Consulting Pipeline (Weeks 5-12, parallel)

Don't wait for the tools to generate leads before pursuing consulting. Run both in parallel.

### 3.1 LinkedIn outreach (manual, no tools)
5 personalised connection requests per day to:
- Brisbane-based SME owners (search by location + title)
- Marketing managers at companies 20-200 staff, Brisbane/QLD
- eCommerce business owners (identify from hashtags, groups)

Follow-up message (sent 3 days after connection accepts):
"Thanks for connecting. I'm building ProjxAI — helping Australian SMEs implement AI practically. Not a pitch — just curious: what's the biggest repetitive process in your business right now that you wish was automated? Genuinely asking because I'm researching where to build next."

Listen, don't sell. Build relationships. The goal is discovery calls, not immediate sales.

### 3.2 Content-led consulting pipeline
Each blog post should include a consulting CTA at the bottom.
Posts target long-tail keywords where the searcher has buying intent:
- "AI consultant Brisbane"
- "how to implement AI in small business Australia"
- "AI strategy for SMEs"

Blog posts drive organic traffic → Work With Us page → discovery calls

### 3.3 Referral activation
Personal network outreach — not cold. Michael personally messages:
- Former colleagues, clients, business contacts
- "I've launched ProjxAI — if you know any business owner curious about AI, I'd love an intro."
Target: 3 referral introductions per week for the first month.

## Phase 4 — Espionage Engine Launch (Weeks 10-16)

### 4.1 Pre-launch waitlist
Before building the full product, validate demand:
- Add waitlist email capture to /tools/competitor-espionage-engine
- Promote via LinkedIn: "Building something for eCommerce businesses who want to know exactly what their competitors are doing with their ads. Launching soon — get early access."
- Target: 50 waitlist signups before launch

### 4.2 Launch sequence
- Email waitlist first (exclusivity)
- LinkedIn announcement with a teaser of what a sample report looks like
- ProductHunt launch (same day as public launch for maximum momentum)

### 4.3 Paid traffic — Espionage Engine
Budget: $200-300 AUD for initial test (from first consulting revenue)
Platform: Meta — eCommerce business owners, 28-50, interested in Facebook Ads, Shopify
Ad angle: Show a blurred/redacted sample report. "We pulled your competitor's entire ad strategy. Want to see it?"
Target CPA: under $20 per purchase (at $99 price, 5:1 ROAS minimum)
